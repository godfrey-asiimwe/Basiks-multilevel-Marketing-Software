Mode of Action

Pylopass is obtained through fermentation of a unique and patented bacteria strain of lactobacillus reuteri DSM17648.

Pylopass has the ability to recognize surface structures on Helibacter pylori and to form co-aggregates.

Co-aggregates are flushed from the body through the gastrointestinal tract.

Pylopass is comprised of stablised bacteria and therefore stable at room temperature.
